Double Agent
By: Brandon DeHart

This is an interactive story

You're an agent who is brought to a secret spy agency. They think they have a double agent on their hands.
You've been tasked to investigate what's going on and find the double-agent and expose him.
You follow him around for the day to see what he does and try to find intel on him.
He discovers your on to him and he sets up a trap in the headquarters. 

You use your PIN and CheckIn time to verify who you are before weapons shoot and kill you.
